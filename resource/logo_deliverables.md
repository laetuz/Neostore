# Logo Design Brief: HoloMarket

## 1. Project Overview

App Name: HoloMarket

Product Description: A dedicated Android marketplace and digital archive serving legacy APKs to early 2010s retro Android devices.

Target Audience: Retro hardware collectors, developers, and Android purists who appreciate the golden era of the OS.

## 2. Brand Identity & Vibe

Theme: Early 2010s Android Stock ROM, utilitarian, nostalgic, and archival.

Design Language: The logo must evoke the Android 4.0 (Ice Cream Sandwich) to 4.3 (Jelly Bean) era. It should reject modern "flat UI" minimalism in favor of the early 2010s aesthetic: subtle gloss, distinct geometry, and slight skeuomorphism. It should look like a system app that shipped natively on a 2012 device.

## 3. Color Palette Guidelines

Base (The Anchor): Use the established Neotica ecosystem primary colors so the logo clearly belongs to the Neotica family of products.

Base Color: #5D6EE0

Background Color: #151A3B

## 4. Visual Concepts & Inspiration
   Please explore directions that blend the Neotica brand identity with early Android motifs.

Design Preference: Skeuomorphic design is highly preferred for the primary logo.

Primary Concept (The Classic Market Bag): A skeuomorphic digital shopping bag, physical package, or software box. It should utilize the Neotica base and background colors as the "branding" or structural colors on the bag itself.

## 5. Deliverables & Scope
   I need two distinct versions of the final logo. (Note for billing: I will count and pay for these as two separate logo deliverables.)

Deliverable 1: The primary skeuomorphic/classic 2012 version.

Deliverable 2: A modernized, flat version of the same icon.
